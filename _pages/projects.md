**[NewtonVR](https://github.com/TomorrowTodayLabs/NewtonVR)**
*Tomorrow Today Labs · 2016*

Co-created a physics-based VR interaction system built in Unity3D for SteamVR/HTC Vive. Implements non-kinematic interactions through Unity's physics engine (PhysX), enabling realistic object grabbing, throwing, and manipulation in room-scale VR.

*Unity · C# · SteamVR · HTC Vive · PhysX*

**WallPlacer (HoloLens)**
*Microsoft Holographic Hackathon · 2016*

Hackathon project for the Microsoft HoloLens. Allows users to place and preview virtual wall art and decorations in their physical space using mixed reality.

*Unity · C# · HoloLens · Mixed Reality*
